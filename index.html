Welcome to GiT Family

Once upon a time...

In 2011, Carola Eijsenring (Indigo-Wereld) developed Get in Touch Program with a vision to support spouses of international employees. And year after year, GiT grew bigger and warmer with her love and support. After 10 long years of her nurturing, I (Megha Vaidya, Kuroosh), as her successor, look forward to carrying the torch forward with same enthusiasm...

Project

We have discussion for making new project, one project after other one...

we have been preparing for a photo-exhibition that is planned to take place in June. After talking with Anke, Irma and Maxime last week, we came up with few new ideas to gather materials on. So, until May 14, we will be working together in collecting the materials. On our upcoming three consecutive Thursdays, we will, therefore, discuss and update over what have been shared already and what we still need to collect in coming days. Details sent by Anke will be shared with you all. If you would like to participate in any way that fits you, you are very welcome.
