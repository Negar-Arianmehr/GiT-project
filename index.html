Welcome to GiT Family

Once upon a time...

In 2011, Carola Eijsenring (Indigo-Wereld) developed Get in Touch Program with a vision to support spouses of international employees. And year after year, GiT grew bigger and warmer with her love and support. After 10 long years of her nurturing, I (Megha Vaidya, Kuroosh), as her successor, look forward to carrying the torch forward with same enthusiasm...

Activities

MEETING IN PARkS

Once again, it’s time to go out, meet a friend, make your own plan, and enjoy your day. Since it’s all upto the pair what, when and where, please feel free to plan according to your convenience. All that matters is you have a wonderful time with a friend.

JIGSAW PUZZLE

Last year we had a great time playing Jigsaw puzzle together online. Coming week, once again it is time for jigsaw puzzle. We can create a Private Room and select a picture along with the level of difficulty.

WEDDING CELEBRATIONS

In the past, we had beautiful presentations on wedding celebrations from different parts of the world. Starting with wedding celebration in Nepal, we journeyed towards India, Brazil, Colombia, and Iran. And coming week, our dear Marcela will be sharing about the Christian wedding culture and celebration in Colombia.
